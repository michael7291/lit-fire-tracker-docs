Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Lit - Financial Independence Progress Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Financial Data: Account balances, investment amounts, expenses, and other financial information you manually enter or connect via Plaid
  • Account Information: Email address and authentication information when you create an account
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and app performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate your FIRE progress and financial projections
  • Provide personalized financial insights and recommendations
  • Sync your data across devices when you're logged in
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Process subscription payments and manage your premium account
  • Send important updates about your account or the app

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information both in transit and at rest. Your financial data is stored in a secure database and is only accessible to you when you're logged in.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For data storage, authentication, and analytics (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions and purchases
  • Plaid: For securely connecting your bank accounts (if you choose to use this feature)
  • Apple App Store: For processing subscription payments

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them to understand how they handle your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share your data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide the app's functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
